## Full description — max 4000 chars

```
Solitaire Tower is a clean, ad-free take on Klondike — designed for players who want the puzzle, not the pop-ups.

HOW TO PLAY
• Klondike Draw 1 — flip one card from stock per turn
• Klondike Draw 3 — flip three cards each turn, for the harder ruleset
• Drag single cards or full stacks; valid drops snap, invalid ones bounce back
• Stuck? One-tap hint, and auto-complete finishes a solved board for you

EVERY DAY, A NEW DEAL
• Daily Challenge — one fresh deal each day, identical for every player
• Streak counter rewards consecutive daily wins
• Lifetime stats track wins, losses, fastest time, and best score per ruleset

WHY YOU'LL LIKE IT
★ No ads. No in-app purchases. No paywalls.
★ Snapshot auto-save — close the app mid-hand, resume exactly where you left off
★ Deterministic deal codes — every game has a code you can share to play the same layout with a friend
★ Unlimited undo
★ Win celebration — cards cascade across the table, confetti rains down
★ 9 achievements — first win, win without hints, win without undo, 3-minute speed run, 100 wins, daily streaks, and more
★ Localized in Korean and English
★ Tap-to-share game codes; copy-to-clipboard for victory stats

PRIVACY
Solitaire Tower uses Firebase (by Google) only for anonymous save sync, crash diagnostics, and aggregate usage analytics. No personal data is collected, no advertising SDKs, no data sales. Full details in the privacy policy.

Built by a solo developer at Studio Mangru. Made with care, played with a single thumb. Feedback and bug reports welcome at mangru.studio@gmail.com.
```
*(approximately 1,500 chars — well under the 4,000 limit, leaves room for seasonal updates and post-launch modes)*
